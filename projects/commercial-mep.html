LOD 400 Fabrication Level

Commercial Facility MEP Coordination

A deep dive into high-density MEP routing and automated clash resolution for a 200k sq. ft. facility.

The Challenge

The project involved a highly constrained ceiling plenum with overlapping HVAC ductwork, fire protection lines, and complex electrical containment. The primary goal was to ensure zero on-site halts for a fast-track construction schedule.

My Technical Approach

  • Federated Modeling: Consolidated multi-trade models using Navisworks Manage and ACC (BIM 360).
  • Automated Clash Detection: Developed custom clash matrices to prioritize "Hard Clashes" over clearance issues.
  • Prefabrication Readiness: Modeled all systems at LOD 400 to facilitate off-site spooling.
